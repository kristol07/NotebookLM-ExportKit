Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-09

NotebookLM ExportKit is a browser extension that helps you export NotebookLM content into formats like CSV, JSON, Markdown, and more. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address when you sign in with a one-time code.
  • Google sign-in data: basic Google profile info and email when you sign in with Google.
  • Google OAuth tokens: access tokens used to upload files to your Google Drive when enabled.
  • Subscription data: plan status, subscription status, and billing period end for Plus access.
  • Trial usage: a counter of how many free trial exports you have used.
  • Support data: any information you send to us when you contact support.
  • Technical data: standard server logs (e.g., IP address) handled by our service providers.

What we do not collect

  • NotebookLM content is processed locally in your browser and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not store your Google Drive files or their contents on our servers.
  • We do not run advertising or analytics trackers in the extension.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • Upload exports to Google Drive when you choose Drive as the destination.
  • Enable Plus features, manage trials, and show subscription status.
  • Provide billing support and resolve account issues.
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.

Payments and third-party services

We use Supabase for authentication and subscription metadata storage. We use Creem to process payments and manage subscriptions. We use Google APIs to upload exports to your Google Drive when enabled. Payment details (such as card numbers) are handled directly by Creem and are not stored by us.

Data retention

We retain account and subscription data while your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting support. Some records may be retained as required for legal or billing purposes.

Your choices

  • You can use the extension without signing in for free exports.
  • You can choose Download or Google Drive as the export destination.
  • You can disconnect Google Drive by signing out of Google in the app or revoking access in your Google account.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription from the billing portal.
  • You can request account deletion via support.

Children's privacy

NotebookLM ExportKit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions about privacy, contact us at support@linknows.fun.
