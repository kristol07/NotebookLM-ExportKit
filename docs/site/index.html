FAQ

Is my data stored?

Exports are generated in your browser. Files are saved locally unless you send them to Google Drive or Notion.

How do trials and billing work?

Advanced exports require login and include a limited number of trials. After trials are used, a $4.99/month subscription keeps advanced exports available.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome is the primary target. Edge and Firefox builds are planned.

Does this work on any NotebookLM content?

Supported today: chats, quizzes, flashcards, mindmaps, notes, reports, source lists, and data tables.
